FAQ
How do I find a blood donor?
Click on the 'Blood' icon in the services section to view available donors in our area.
Is this service free?
Yes, Raozan Care is a community initiative providing information free of charge.
How can I update or delete Donor Profile?
Click on the 'Blood' icon then on top corner you will see 'Register' icon, below you can update or delete your status or profile.
Our Journey
Raozan Care was established in 2020 with a mission to bridge the gap between emergency service providers and the citizens of Raozan. Our goal is to ensure that no one in our community feels helpless during a crisis.
