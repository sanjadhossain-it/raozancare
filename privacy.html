Our Commitment

We are dedicated to protecting your data while managing humanitarian services. This policy details how Raozan Care handles your information.

Information We Collect

  • Donors: Name, Age, Blood Group, and Location.
  • Tutors: Educational credentials and ID (via WhatsApp verification).
  • Tech Data: IP address and device type for security logs.

Female Donor Safety

We implement a strict "Zero-Public-Exposure" policy for female donors:

Restricted Visibility

Female contact details are hidden from the public. Only verified System Admins can access this data to facilitate emergency requests manually.

Tutor Verification

To maintain platform integrity, Tutors must submit verification documents via WhatsApp. Profiles are only activated after manual review by our staff.

Ads & Analytics

We use Google AdSense and Analytics to sustain our free services. These tools use cookies (DART) to show relevant content. You can opt-out via Google's Ad settings.

Data Deletion

You own your data. You can request a full account deletion at any time. Once requested, your records are purged from our servers within 72 hours.
