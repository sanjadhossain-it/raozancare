Donor Registration

1. Eligibility: By registering, you confirm you are at least 18 years old and weigh at least 50kg.

2. Privacy: Your phone number and blood group will be accessible to medical personnel and verified seekers.

3. Health Disclosure: You agree to provide truthful information regarding your medical history and recent illnesses.

4. Consent: You consent to receive notifications via SMS or call regarding urgent blood requirements.
